About the Founder

Hi, I’m Pratikshya Sahoo, the founder of BrandVerseDigital — a passionate digital marketing enthusiast with a vision to help green, agri, and eco-centric businesses flourish in the digital age. My journey started with a deep interest in storytelling, content creation, and the psychology of what makes people click, connect, and convert online. With hands-on experience in managing social media, writing compelling content, and optimizing websites, I realized one thing — a strong digital presence isn’t optional anymore; it’s essential. BrandVerseDigital was born out of this belief. I wanted to create a space where sustainability-driven brands, especially those rooted in agriculture, horticulture, and nature-focused lifestyles, could grow their voice, audience, and impact online.
